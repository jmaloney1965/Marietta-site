Privacy Policy

This website is operated by Marietta Research Solutions. We respect your privacy and collect only the information needed to respond to inquiries and provide requested services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (such as name, email address, phone number).
  • Inquiry details you submit (service selection and message content).
  • Basic technical information that may be logged by standard web hosting (e.g., browser type, date/time of access).

How We Use Information

  • To respond to your message and communicate about services.
  • To understand your request and provide support and deliverables.
  • To improve internal workflow and client communications.

Contact Form Processing

Website contact form submissions are processed using a third-party form service (Formspree). When you submit the form, the information you provide is transmitted to that service to deliver your message.

Important: Do not submit sensitive personal data (e.g., SSNs, dates of birth, driver’s license numbers, financial account numbers) or confidential legal strategy through the website form. If documents are needed, we will provide a secure method to share files.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to deliver requested services, coordinate with contractors supporting your engagement, or comply with law.

Retention

We retain inquiry messages and related communications as needed for ongoing support, recordkeeping, and business operations.

Your Choices

  • You may request that we update or delete your contact information by emailing us.
  • If you prefer not to use the web form, you may contact us directly via phone or email.
