Privacy Policy

Marietta Research Solutions respects your privacy and is committed to handling information responsibly. This page explains what information we collect, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily submit through our contact form or by email, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • The content of your message and service request

How We Use Information

We use submitted information only for business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiry
  • Scheduling consultations or appointments
  • Providing requested services or service information
  • Maintaining basic business records and communications

Contact Forms & Third-Party Processing

Our website contact form may be processed using a third-party service provider (such as Formspree) to deliver messages to our email inbox. If you submit a message through the website, your information may be handled by that provider as part of message delivery.

Confidentiality and Sensitive Information

Please do not submit sensitive personal data, confidential legal strategy, financial account numbers, or highly sensitive documents through public website forms.

If documents are needed for your request, we can provide a more secure method to share files after initial contact.

No Legal Advice / No Attorney-Client Relationship

Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation. Communications through this website, email, or contact form do not create an attorney-client relationship.

Cookies & Analytics

We do not intentionally collect personal information through cookies. However, GitHub Pages or embedded third-party tools may collect limited technical information (such as browser type or basic site traffic data) as part of normal website operation.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide services you request
  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Comply with applicable laws or lawful requests

Data Retention

We retain messages and contact information as needed for business communication, recordkeeping, and service follow-up. If you would like to request deletion of your contact message, you may contact us.
