Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Marietta Research Solutions respects your privacy. This page explains what information may be collected through this website and how it is used.

Information We Collect

If you contact us through the website, we may receive information you voluntarily submit, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • The message you send through the contact form

How We Use Information

We use submitted information only to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Communicate about your request for services
  • Coordinate scheduling and next steps

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your information for marketing purposes
  • We do not create public client lists

Third-Party Services

Our contact form may be powered by a third-party service provider (such as Formspree) to deliver messages to our email. Submitted information is processed by that provider in order to transmit your message.

Confidential Information

Please do not send highly sensitive personal information, confidential legal strategy, financial account numbers, or private documents through the website contact form unless a secure sharing method is arranged.

Data Retention

Messages and related email communications may be retained as needed for business purposes such as follow-up, recordkeeping, and continuity of service.

Disclaimer

Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation. Communications through this website do not create an attorney-client relationship.
