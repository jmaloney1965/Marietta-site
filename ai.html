AI Solutions for Small Businesses & Law Firms

We help small businesses and small law firms apply practical AI tools to save time, reduce manual work, and improve consistency—without complicated “big enterprise” deployments.

What We Build

  • Document automation: intake summaries, draft templates, and structured checklists
  • Knowledge organization: internal “how we do it” playbooks and searchable reference libraries
  • Workflow support: email triage, client intake routing, and repeatable task pipelines
  • Data clean-up: organize spreadsheets and records so your tools work reliably
  • AI adoption training: safe prompts, staff guidelines, and practical best practices
