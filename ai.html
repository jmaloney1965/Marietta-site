AI for Small Businesses & Law Firms

Practical AI support for small organizations that want faster workflows, cleaner documentation, and better client service. We focus on real-world automation you can actually use—without complicated systems or enterprise overhead.

Marietta Research Solutions helps small teams use AI responsibly to save time, improve consistency, and reduce repetitive work. Our focus is practical implementation: templates, workflows, and automation that you can actually use day-to-day.

What We Help With

  • AI workflow design: create repeatable processes for intake, drafts, summaries, and follow-ups
  • Templates & prompt libraries: purpose-built prompts that produce consistent outputs
  • Document workflows: organize, outline, summarize, and standardize documents
  • Automation support: lightweight systems to reduce admin work and improve turnaround time
  • Quality control: practical review checklists to reduce errors and hallucinations

Common Use Cases for Law Firms

  • Client intake assistance: turn raw notes into structured intake summaries
  • Drafting support: first-pass drafting for emails, letters, checklists, and internal memos
  • Document organization: create timelines, issue lists, and document indexes
  • Form & filing support: improve consistency for document formatting and workflow steps
  • Case summaries: transform long documents into concise summaries for review

Common Use Cases for Small Businesses

  • Customer communication: consistent, professional email templates and FAQs
  • Operations: SOP drafts, checklists, and training documentation
  • Marketing support: headlines, service descriptions, landing-page wording
  • Internal research: organize findings into action plans and decision notes
  • Proposal support: outlines and narrative structure for grant/proposal drafts

Deliverables You Can Expect

  • AI prompt sets customized to your business workflows
  • Reusable templates (intake, summaries, emails, checklists)
  • Step-by-step “how to run it” instructions for your team
  • Quality control checklist and safe-use guidance
  • Optional training session (remote) with your team

How Engagements Work

  • Step 1 — Discovery: identify bottlenecks and the workflows that waste time
  • Step 2 — Design: create templates and prompts to match your tasks and tone
  • Step 3 — Implement: test, refine, and hand off a repeatable process
  • Step 4 — Improve: optional follow-up iterations as your workflow evolves

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No legal advice: We do not provide legal advice or legal representation. AI support is operational and administrative.
  • Human review required: AI can produce incorrect or incomplete outputs. Final review is always required.
  • Confidentiality: Do not send sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through public web forms.
  • Privacy considerations: We can recommend best practices for safer workflows, but you control what data is shared.
