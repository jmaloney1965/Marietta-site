Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to common questions about how we work, what we offer, and important limitations. If you don’t see your question here, please reach out via the contact form.

What types of clients do you work with?

We support small businesses, law firms, inventors, and individuals who need organized, professional help with engineering consulting, AI workflow support, document preparation, patent drafting support, SBIR proposal support, prison consulting/reentry planning, and notary services.

Do you offer legal advice?

No. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation. Paralegal/document services are administrative support only.

Are you a patent attorney or patent agent?

No. Patent services are technical drafting and documentation support intended to help you prepare materials for review by a registered patent attorney or patent agent.

Can you guarantee outcomes (court results, funding, approvals, jobs, housing, etc.)?

No. We can help you prepare, organize, and present information clearly, but we cannot guarantee outcomes that depend on third parties, agencies, courts, or employers.

How does the consultation process work?

We start with a brief consultation to understand your goals and the deliverables you need. Then we agree on scope, timeline, and what you will provide. We deliver work in clear drafts, with the option to refine.

How quickly do you respond?

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

What about confidentiality?

Please do not send sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through the public website form. If documents are needed, we can provide a safer method to share files after initial contact.

Do you provide notary services?

Yes, by appointment in Marietta, GA. Signers must be present with valid ID. Notary services do not include drafting, legal interpretation, or advice.

Where are you located?
