FAQ

Do you provide legal advice?

No. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice or legal representation. Paralegal/document services are non-legal support only.

Do you work with small law firms?

Yes. We support small firms with workflow help, document organization, intake structure, and practical AI adoption—always with attorney review for any legal content.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Many engagements are remote via email/video call. Notary services are by appointment.

How quickly can you start?

Typically within 1–2 business days after an initial consult, depending on scope and schedule.

What should I send to get started?

A short description of your goal, any relevant background, your timeline, and how you prefer to be contacted. Please do not send sensitive personal data through the website form.

Do you sign NDAs?

Often yes, depending on the engagement. We can discuss confidentiality needs during the initial consult.

Do you guarantee outcomes (awards, approvals, results)?

No. We provide professional support and best-effort deliverables, but outcomes depend on third parties, constraints, data, and implementation.
