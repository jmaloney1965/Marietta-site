FAQ

Answers to common questions about services, how engagements work, and what to expect. If you don’t see your question, please contact us.

Do you provide legal advice or legal representation?

No. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation. Paralegal/document services are non-legal support only. Any legal strategy or advice must be provided by a licensed attorney.

What types of clients do you work with?

We support small businesses, law firms, inventors, and individuals. Many engagements are remote, with in-person services available by appointment when appropriate (including notary services).

Do you work with small law firms on AI adoption?

Yes. We help small firms adopt practical AI workflows for intake/triage, drafting assistance, internal knowledge organization, and process automation—always with attorney review for legal content.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Many services are delivered remotely via email and video calls. Notary services are by appointment.

How quickly can you start?

We typically respond within 1–2 business days and can often begin shortly after a brief consult, depending on scope and scheduling.

What do you need from me to get started?

A short description of your goal, relevant background, timeline, and your preferred contact method. Please do not send sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through the website form. If documents are needed, we can provide a secure method to share files.

Do you sign NDAs?

Often, yes—depending on the engagement. We can discuss confidentiality needs and documentation during the initial consult.

Do you guarantee outcomes (approval, award, business results)?

No. We provide professional support and best-effort deliverables, but outcomes can depend on third parties, requirements, data, and implementation.

Do you provide notary services?

Yes, by appointment in Marietta, GA. Notary services do not include document drafting, legal interpretation, or advice.

What engagement options do you offer?

Common options include a consultation, hourly support, fixed-price packages with defined deliverables, and ongoing support. If you contact us with your goals and timeline, we’ll suggest a structure that fits.
