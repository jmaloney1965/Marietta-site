Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Below are common questions about Marietta Research Solutions. If your situation is unique or time-sensitive, please reach out using the Contact / Consult page.

1) What services do you offer?

We provide specialized support services including:

  • Electrical Engineering Consulting
  • AI for Small Businesses & Law Firms
  • Paralegal Support & Document Preparation (non-legal support)
  • Patent Drafting Support (non-legal support)
  • SBIR Grant Writing Support
  • Prison Consulting & Reentry Support
  • Notary Public Services (by appointment)

2) Do you offer free consultations?

We typically start with a short intake message or call to understand your needs. If extended consultation time is required, we will confirm expectations before proceeding.

3) What is your typical response time?

We typically respond within 1–2 business days. Urgent requests can sometimes be accommodated depending on availability.

4) How do engagements usually work?

  • Step 1: You send a request describing your goals and timeline
  • Step 2: We clarify scope, deliverables, turnaround, and any needed inputs
  • Step 3: Work begins and deliverables are provided for review

5) Are you a law firm?

No. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation.

6) Do you provide legal advice?

No. We do not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or legal interpretation. Paralegal/document services are non-legal support only. If you need legal advice, you should consult a licensed attorney.

7) Can you help with patent filings?

We can assist with technical writing support and invention organization, but we do not provide legal advice. Patent filing decisions, claim strategy, and submission should be handled by a registered patent attorney or agent.

8) Do you offer notary services?

Yes — notary services are available by appointment in Marietta, GA. Notary work does not include drafting documents or providing legal interpretation.

9) Can I upload sensitive documents through your contact form?

Please do not send sensitive personal data, confidential legal strategy, or private documents through the contact form. If documents are needed, we will reply with a secure way to share files.
