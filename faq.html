FAQ

Quick answers to common questions. If you don’t see your question here, contact us.

Do you provide legal advice or legal representation?

No. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm. Paralegal/document services are non-legal support only. Any legal advice must come from a licensed attorney.

What’s the best way to start?

Use the contact form or email with your goal, timeline, and the service area. We’ll reply with next steps and a proposed scope.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. Many engagements are remote-friendly. Notary services are by appointment.

How quickly do you respond?

Typically within 1–2 business days.

Can I send documents through the contact form?

Please do not send sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through the web form. If documents are needed, we will provide a secure method to share files.

Do you guarantee outcomes?

No. We provide professional support and deliverables, but outcomes depend on factors outside our control (reviewers, agencies, counsel decisions, testing, timelines, etc.).

What about AI use for law firms?

We help implement practical workflows, but AI output must be reviewed by a licensed attorney. We do not provide legal advice or representation.
