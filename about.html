About Marietta Research Solutions

Marietta Research Solutions is a small professional services firm based in Marietta, Georgia supporting individuals, inventors, and small businesses with clear, one-to-one solutions.

Our approach is practical and personal: we help clients define the real problem, organize requirements, and produce high-quality deliverables that are ready for action.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help individuals and small businesses meet their needs with one-to-one personalized solutions.
