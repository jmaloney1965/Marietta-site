About Marietta Research Solutions

Marietta Research Solutions provides personalized professional support for individuals, small businesses, law firms, and inventors. We focus on clear communication, well-organized deliverables, and practical solutions that reduce friction and save time.

Our Mission

To help individuals and small businesses meet their needs with one-to-one, personalized solutions—delivered with professionalism, discretion, and a focus on practical results.

What Makes Us Different

  • Personalized support: you work directly with us, not a call center.
  • Clear scope: we define deliverables and timelines up front.
  • Strong documentation: organized outputs designed to be useful and reusable.
  • Flexible resourcing: when appropriate, we leverage specialized contractors to match your needs.

Primary Service Areas

  • AI workflow support for small businesses and law firms
  • Paralegal/document preparation support (non-legal)
