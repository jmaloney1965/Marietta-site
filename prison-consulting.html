Prison Consulting & Reentry Support

Structured support for individuals and families navigating incarceration, release preparation, and reentry planning. We provide non-legal assistance focused on organization, documentation, and practical next steps.

Marietta Research Solutions provides practical, one-to-one support for individuals and families navigating incarceration-related administration and reentry planning. We focus on organization, readiness, and non-legal assistance with paperwork—so you can take the next steps with clarity.

How We Can Help

  • Reentry planning: step-by-step readiness checklists, timelines, and action planning
  • Paperwork organization: gather, label, and organize records into a clean packet
  • Form preparation assistance: help completing forms using your provided information
  • Letter and document formatting: clean, professional formatting for personal statements and support letters
  • Resource navigation: identify common resource categories (IDs, housing, employment, training)

Common Support Areas

  • Identification readiness: checklist for documents needed to obtain ID after release
  • Employment preparation: resume formatting, basic job-search structure, and readiness checklist
  • Housing planning: organization of contact lists, application steps, and documentation
  • Family coordination: shared plan and document packet so everyone is aligned
  • Administrative follow-through: reminders and task lists to reduce missed steps

What You Receive

  • A clear plan with prioritized next steps
  • An organized document packet (digital or printable)
  • Checklists tailored to your specific situation and timeline
  • Drafted/cleaned documents for your review (based on your inputs)
  • Optional follow-up session to revise the plan as circumstances change

How Engagements Work

  • Step 1 — Intake: quick consultation to understand goals, deadlines, and constraints
  • Step 2 — Organize: gather and structure documents, facts, and requirements
  • Step 3 — Build: create a plan, checklists, and prepared document drafts
  • Step 4 — Review: confirm accuracy with you and finalize deliverables

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No legal advice or representation. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or legal representation.
  • No outcome guarantees. We cannot guarantee approval of requests, services, housing, employment, or any legal/court outcome.
  • Client-provided information. We prepare documents and plans based on the information you provide.
  • Confidentiality. Do not submit sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through public web forms. Request a secure file-sharing method if needed.
  • Emergency situations. If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, contact local emergency services.
