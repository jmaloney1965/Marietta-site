Notary Public Services

Notary services are available by appointment in Marietta, Georgia. Contact us to coordinate time, location, and document requirements.

Before Your Appointment

  • Bring a valid government-issued photo ID.
  • Bring the complete document(s) to be notarized.
  • Do not sign documents in advance if notarization requires witnessing.
  • Confirm whether witnesses are required for your document type.

Important Limitations

  • Notary services do not include legal advice or legal representation.
  • Notaries cannot draft documents, interpret legal terms, or advise what you should sign.
