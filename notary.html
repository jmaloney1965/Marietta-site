Notary Public Services

Notary services are available by appointment at our business address in Marietta, Georgia. We prioritize clear communication and a smooth, professional signing experience.

What to Bring

  • Valid photo ID: government-issued identification
  • Unsigned documents: bring documents ready to sign (do not sign in advance)
  • All signers present: each signer must appear with valid ID

Common Notarizations

  • Affidavits
  • Power of attorney (signing only; no advice)
  • Consent forms
  • Business documents

Important Disclaimers

No legal advice. Notary services do not include legal advice, document drafting, or interpretation. We cannot tell you what to sign or which document you need.

Identification required. Notarization requires compliant identification and signer appearance per state rules.
