Notary Public Services

Professional notary services by appointment in Marietta, Georgia. We provide reliable notarizations for common documents with clear scheduling and a respectful client experience.

Marietta Research Solutions offers professional, by-appointment notary services in Marietta, Georgia. Our notary services are intended to help individuals and small businesses complete document execution requirements efficiently and properly.

Service Area & Appointments

  • Location: Marietta, GA (Cobb County)
  • Availability: By appointment only
  • On-site / mobile: Availability may vary (ask during scheduling)

What to Bring

  • Valid government-issued photo ID (required)
  • Your unsigned document(s) — do not sign ahead of time unless instructed by the receiving party
  • All required signers must be present at the appointment
  • Witnesses if your document requires them (ask the receiving party if unsure)

Common Documents We Notarize

  • Affidavits
  • Authorization letters
  • Medical/records release forms
  • General business documents requiring notarization
  • Other documents requiring identity verification and acknowledgment

What We Can and Cannot Do

  • We can: verify identity, witness signing, and complete notarial certificates when appropriate.
  • We cannot: draft documents for you, choose the correct notarization type, or provide legal advice.
  • We cannot: explain legal consequences of signing or interpret legal meaning of documents.

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No legal advice. Notary services do not include legal advice, legal interpretation, or representation.
  • Signer must be present. Documents cannot be notarized without the signer present and properly identified.
  • Document review. We may confirm the document is complete for notarization (e.g., no blank signature spaces), but we do not verify factual accuracy.
  • Fees and requirements. Fees and acceptable ID requirements may vary. Confirm details when scheduling.
