Paralegal Support & Document Preparation

We provide structured, professional document support services for individuals and small law firms, focused on organization, formatting, and non-legal administrative assistance.

  • Document formatting, proofreading, and clean presentation
  • Case file organization (chronologies, indexes, exhibit lists)
  • Drafting support for letters and non-legal templates
  • Scanning, organization, and packaging for attorney review

No legal advice. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or legal representation. Paralegal services are non-legal support only.
