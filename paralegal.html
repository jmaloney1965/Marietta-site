Paralegal Support & Document Preparation

Professional document organization and preparation support for individuals and small firms. We help improve clarity, structure, and readiness for attorney review or filing.

Common Support Areas

  • Form preparation assistance (client-provided information organized clearly)
  • Document formatting, proofreading (non-substantive), and cleanup
  • Case file organization, timelines, checklists, exhibit labeling
  • Administrative support and coordination
  • Templates for repeatable workflows and documents

How It Works

  • 1) Intake: clarify goals and what documents exist
  • 2) Plan: define deliverables and timeline
  • 3) Prepare: organize and format documents
  • 4) Review: you/attorney finalize decisions and content
