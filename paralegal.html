Paralegal Support & Document Preparation

We provide organized, detail-oriented paralegal support and document preparation assistance for attorneys, small firms, and self-represented individuals (where permitted). Services focus on administrative and procedural support—never legal advice.

Support Areas

  • Document preparation: formatting, proofreading, and assembling filings
  • Forms assistance: help completing standard forms using your provided information
  • Case organization: timelines, exhibit lists, and document indexing
  • Client intake: questionnaires, intake summaries, and document checklists
  • Notary coordination: scheduling and notarization support (where applicable)

Designed for Small Firms

Overflow Support

When you’re busy, we help keep filings, drafts, and checklists moving.

Consistency

Templates and standardized formatting to reduce rework.

Client Experience

Clear intake steps and follow-ups that help clients stay on track.

Important Disclaimers

No legal advice or representation. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We do not represent clients in court or provide attorney services. Any legal decisions or strategy must be handled by a licensed attorney.

Forms assistance only. We can help you organize information and complete standard forms, but we cannot tell you what to file, how to argue a case, or what the law requires for your situation.
