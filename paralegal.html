Paralegal Support & Document Preparation

Marietta Research Solutions provides professional, non-legal support to help law firms and individuals stay organized, meet deadlines, and present clean, consistent documents. Our work focuses on administrative and document-production tasks—not legal advice.

How We Support Law Firms

  • Document formatting: consistent fonts, headings, pagination, exhibits, and clean layouts
  • Proofreading & QC: grammar, clarity, consistency, missing sections, and citation checks (non-substantive)
  • Case organization: timelines, document indexes, exhibit lists, and file organization
  • Draft preparation support: assemble attorney-provided content into polished drafts
  • Workflow support: checklists, intake forms, and repeatable templates for common matters

How We Support Individuals

  • Form preparation assistance: help completing forms using your provided information
  • Document organization: compile records, organize attachments, and label supporting documents
  • Formatting help: type, format, and clean documents you provide
  • Administrative guidance: provide procedural checklists and “what to gather” lists (non-legal)

What We Provide

  • Polished, consistent documents prepared from your inputs
  • Organized packets (forms + attachments) ready for your review
  • Checklists for document gathering and submission readiness
  • Templates for intake, timelines, summaries, and internal workflows

Typical Requests

  • Convert rough notes into a structured summary for attorney review
  • Organize evidence into an exhibit list and labeled PDF set
  • Clean up formatting and ensure consistent styles across filings
  • Create a timeline from provided records and correspondence
  • Build reusable templates for letters, checklists, and intake workflows

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No legal advice or representation. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or representation.
  • No attorney-client relationship. Communications do not create an attorney-client relationship.
  • Client-provided information. We prepare documents based only on the information you provide and your instructions.
  • Confidentiality. Do not submit sensitive personal data or confidential legal strategy through public web forms. Request a secure sharing method if documents are needed.
  • Notary limitations. If notary services are requested, notarization does not include drafting, legal interpretation, or advice.
