SBIR Grant Writing Support

We help small businesses prepare strong technical narratives and organized proposal content for SBIR/STTR-style submissions. The focus is clear writing, logical structure, and technical credibility.

Support Includes

  • Technical approach writing: objectives, methods, milestones
  • Work plan structure: tasks, schedule, risks, and mitigations
  • Executive-friendly clarity: translate complex tech into readable sections
  • Editing & compliance help: formatting, consistency, and checklist management

Typical Deliverables

Outline + Draft

A structured draft that aligns with the solicitation and evaluation criteria.

Technical Refinement

Strengthen assumptions, logic, and test/validation plans.

Final Polish

Proofing, formatting, and “make it coherent” cleanup before submission.
