SBIR Grant Writing Support

Structured support for SBIR/STTR proposal development, including technical writing, organization, and compliance checks. We help small businesses present a clear, credible plan with strong technical justification and deliverables.

Marietta Research Solutions supports small businesses pursuing SBIR/STTR opportunities by helping you turn your technical concept into a clear, competitive proposal. We focus on structure, clarity, and technical storytelling—so reviewers can quickly understand your innovation, approach, and impact.

What We Help With

  • Proposal structure & outlines: create a clean section-by-section plan aligned to the solicitation
  • Technical narrative drafting: convert engineering notes into readable, reviewer-friendly content
  • Work plans: tasks, milestones, deliverables, and timeline organization
  • Commercialization support (non-legal): value proposition, market framing, and go-to-market narrative
  • Editing & compliance checks: tighten language, improve flow, and reduce ambiguity
  • Graphics support: help planning figures, tables, and diagrams that strengthen clarity

Typical Deliverables

  • Proposal outline aligned to the agency topic and scoring criteria
  • Drafted or revised technical narrative sections
  • Work plan: tasks, milestones, and measurable deliverables
  • Risk/mitigation table and validation plan (as appropriate)
  • Final proofreading pass for clarity and consistency

Common Client Scenarios

  • First-time SBIR teams: you need structure, templates, and a realistic plan
  • Busy founders: you have the innovation but need writing bandwidth and organization
  • Technical teams: you need the story to be clear to non-specialist reviewers
  • Re-submissions: you want to improve clarity and address reviewer concerns

How Engagements Work

  • Step 1 — Topic alignment: confirm the solicitation requirements and your technical fit
  • Step 2 — Plan: build the outline and define work packages, milestones, and deliverables
  • Step 3 — Draft: write/refine the narrative with clear technical justification
  • Step 4 — Review: tighten language, improve compliance, and finalize submission readiness

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No guarantee of award. Funding decisions are made by the agency; we cannot guarantee results.
  • Client responsibility. You are responsible for submission, accuracy, and meeting all deadlines.
  • No legal or accounting services. We do not provide legal advice, tax advice, or accounting services. Budget guidance is limited to formatting/organization and narrative clarity.
  • Confidentiality. Do not submit proprietary details or sensitive data through public web forms. Request a secure file-sharing method after we reply.
