Fragmented Blade Antennas

Coming Soon

Marietta Research Solutions is developing a line of high-performance fragmented aperture blade antennas covering 2.4–7.5 GHz — spanning WiFi 6E, UWB, and Sub-6 5G bands. Each design is generated using our proprietary AI optimization pipeline and validated with full-wave FDTD electromagnetic simulation.

Technology

Fragmented aperture antennas replace conventional patch or dipole geometries with an AI-optimized metallic fragment pattern on a planar substrate. This approach unlocks wideband performance, compact form factors, and multi-band coverage that conventional designs cannot achieve. Every antenna in the product line is drawn from a database of over 16 million simulated designs.

Stock Designs

Pre-validated antenna designs available as instantly downloadable Gerber files for a fixed fee. Place the design directly on your product PCB or fabricate as a standalone antenna — ready to integrate immediately.
