Electrical Engineering Consulting

Marietta Research Solutions provides high-quality electrical engineering consulting for businesses, inventors, and organizations who need practical technical guidance, analysis, and documentation. We support both early-stage R&D and real-world implementation.

Core Capabilities

  • Antenna engineering: broadband antennas, compact antennas, array concepts, and integration support
  • Telecommunications systems: RF concepts, architectures, and performance trade studies
  • Numerical modeling & simulation: electromagnetic modeling support and interpretation of results
  • Radar concepts & signatures: technical consulting related to radar system concepts and RCS reduction approaches
  • Design documentation: clear requirements, test plans, and engineering writeups suitable for teams and stakeholders
  • Technical review: second-opinion reviews of designs, proposals, or technical decisions

How We Help

We work best when you need a trusted technical partner who can translate complex engineering topics into clear next steps. Engagements can be structured as a one-time consult, short project support, or ongoing technical advising.

Engineering Support

Design guidance, technical problem-solving, and performance tradeoffs for RF and system-level work.

Technical Documentation

Requirements, design summaries, review notes, and written engineering deliverables that reduce risk and keep teams aligned.

R&D / Proposal Support

Technical content support for research proposals including SBIR-style narratives and technical sections.

Typical Clients

  • Small businesses and startups needing specialized engineering insight
  • Inventors preparing technical materials for patent filings or development
  • Teams needing assistance interpreting modeling results or technical tradeoffs
  • Organizations preparing technical proposals and grant submissions

What to Expect

  • Initial consult: brief call or message exchange to clarify goals and timeline
  • Scope & estimate: clear deliverables and time expectations
  • Work product: concise technical notes, calculations, diagrams, or guidance
  • Follow-up: optional review meeting to confirm next steps
