Electrical Engineering Consulting

Practical engineering support for clients who need clear analysis, documentation, and actionable recommendations. Engagements are available remotely and are scoped to your goals, timeline, and constraints.

Typical Areas of Support

  • RF / Antennas: concept review, requirements translation, design support
  • Modeling & Analysis: numerical modeling support, interpretation of results
  • Technical Review: independent review of designs, test results, or documentation
  • Engineering Writing: reports, requirements, whitepapers, and technical summaries
  • Telecommunications Systems: architecture support and planning

How It Works

  • 1) Intake: you share goals, context, and constraints
  • 2) Scope: we define deliverables and timeline
  • 3) Work: analysis + documentation + recommendations
  • 4) Review: walk-through and optional refinements

Engagement Options

  • Consultation: 30–60 minute call
  • Hourly support: scoped engineering tasks
  • Fixed-price package: defined deliverables + schedule
