Electrical Engineering Consulting

Practical electrical engineering consulting for antennas, RF systems, numerical modeling, and telecommunications. We deliver clear documentation, measurable technical outputs, and professional turnaround.

Marietta Research Solutions provides practical, one-to-one engineering support for clients who need clear technical insight, organized documentation, and high-quality deliverables. We work with small businesses, inventors, and teams that need reliable engineering assistance without overhead.

Core Engineering Expertise

  • Antennas & RF systems: broadband antennas, array concepts, feeds, integration support
  • Electromagnetic modeling: numerical methods, simulation support, interpretation of results
  • Telecommunications systems: system-level performance considerations and trade studies
  • RCS concepts & reduction approaches: conceptual analysis and technical communication
  • Technical writing: structured engineering reports, diagrams, summaries, and presentations

What We Can Deliver

  • Clear engineering notes and recommendations
  • Trade studies and decision-ready technical summaries
  • Specifications and requirements support (drafting + refinement)
  • Design review preparation and technical Q&A support
  • Well-organized documentation suitable for stakeholders or collaborators

Typical Use Cases

  • Small companies: get experienced engineering guidance for proposals or early product work
  • Inventors: translate an idea into structured technical documentation
  • Teams under time pressure: add engineering bandwidth for review, writing, or analysis
  • Grant and R&D support: provide engineering detail to strengthen SBIR/STTR submissions

How Engagements Work

  • Step 1 — Scope: We clarify deliverables, timeline, and priority requirements.
  • Step 2 — Work: We develop organized content, analysis, or documentation as agreed.
  • Step 3 — Review: You receive clear deliverables and can request refinement.
