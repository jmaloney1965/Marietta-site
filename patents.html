Patent Drafting Support

Technical drafting support for inventors and engineering teams preparing patent materials. We help organize invention disclosures, technical descriptions, figures, and supporting documentation for attorney review.

Marietta Research Solutions helps inventors and technical teams translate complex ideas into clear, organized technical documentation that supports patent preparation. We provide structured drafting support, invention disclosure organization, and technical writing that makes it easier to work with a registered patent attorney or agent.

What We Help With

  • Invention disclosure support: organize the idea, novelty, and design rationale
  • Technical drafting assistance: convert engineering notes into clean, structured narrative
  • System descriptions: components, interfaces, workflows, and use cases
  • Embodiments & variations: identify alternate implementations and fallback options
  • Figures support: provide figure lists, callouts, and descriptions for drawings/diagrams
  • Prior-art discussion prep: summarize your known references for attorney review

Typical Deliverables

  • Structured invention disclosure document (ready for attorney intake)
  • Technical narrative describing the invention and how it works
  • Alternative embodiments and design variations
  • Feature list and terminology definitions
  • Figure captions and figure callout tables (for drawings support)

Common Client Scenarios

  • Inventors: you have an idea but need help organizing it into a coherent disclosure
  • Startups: you need patent-ready technical documentation while moving fast
  • Engineering teams: convert internal notes into clean external-quality documentation
  • Attorney collaboration: reduce attorney time spent extracting technical details

How the Process Works

  • Step 1 — Intake: brief call + review of your concept, notes, and supporting files
  • Step 2 — Structure: outline the invention with key features and differentiators
  • Step 3 — Draft: write a clean technical narrative with embodiments/variations
  • Step 4 — Review: iterate based on your feedback and prepare a final draft package
  • Step 5 — Handoff: deliver the package for patent attorney/agent review and filing

Important Notes / Disclaimers

  • No legal advice. Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation.
  • Not a patent attorney or agent. We do not provide legal opinions on patentability, infringement, claim scope, or filing strategy.
  • Attorney review recommended. Final patent applications should be reviewed by a registered patent attorney or patent agent.
  • Client responsibility. You are responsible for your filing decisions, disclosures, and deadlines.
  • Confidentiality. Do not send sensitive or proprietary information through public web forms. Request a secure file-sharing method after we reply.
