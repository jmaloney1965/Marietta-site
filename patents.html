Patent Drafting Support

We support inventors and small businesses with technical writing and drafting assistance for patent-related documents, especially in engineering and technology domains. Our work emphasizes clarity, completeness, and consistent structure to help you and your counsel move efficiently.

What We Can Help With

  • Invention disclosures: structured write-ups, background, and differentiators
  • Technical descriptions: system overviews, components, and implementation details
  • Figures support: figure descriptions and labeling suggestions (non-legal)
  • Prior art summary support: organize and summarize references you provide
  • Review & refinement: improve readability and consistency of draft content

Best Fit

Engineering-heavy inventions

RF systems, antennas, telecom, modeling, and related technical domains.

Busy inventors

Turn rough notes into a structured, readable technical narrative.

Small teams

Support your attorney with clear technical explanations and details.

Important Disclaimers

Not a law firm; no legal advice. We do not provide legal advice, determine patentability, interpret patent law, or represent clients before the USPTO. We provide technical writing and drafting assistance only.

Attorney review recommended. Patent filings should be reviewed and finalized by a registered patent attorney or agent.
