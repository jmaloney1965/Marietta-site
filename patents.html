Patent Drafting Support

We provide technical writing support to help inventors and teams create clear invention documentation suitable for attorney review and patent filing preparation.

Typical Deliverables

  • Structured invention disclosure write-ups from your technical notes
  • Technical background and description of embodiments (non-legal)
  • Figure descriptions and labeling guidance
  • Consistency checks and document organization for counsel review

How It Works

  • 1) Intake: you share description, sketches, and reference materials
  • 2) Outline: we structure the disclosure
  • 3) Draft: develop technical sections and figure descriptions
  • 4) Review: you/counsel finalize content and filing decisions
