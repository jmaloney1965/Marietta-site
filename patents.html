Patent Drafting Support

  • Invention disclosure organization and refinement
  • Technical descriptions and background sections
  • Claim-supporting technical detail development
  • Figure labeling guidance and drafting support
  • Consistency checks across terminology and embodiments

Disclaimer: Marietta Research Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Patent filings should be reviewed and submitted by a registered professional.
